19th & Early 20th Century

1830 – French Colonization of Algeria

France began its colonial campaign in North Africa by invading and occupying Algeria, initiating a long period of control.

1870–1871 – Franco-Prussian War

France was defeated by Prussia, leading to the fall of Napoleon III and the establishment of the French Third Republic.

1880–1914 – Colonial Expansion

France expanded its global empire significantly, especially in Africa and Southeast Asia, making it one of the largest colonial powers.

1914–1918 – World War I

France played a major role in World War I as part of the Allied Powers. The war devastated much of northern France but ended in Allied victory.
